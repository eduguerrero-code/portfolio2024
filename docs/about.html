My background

I'm Edu, a Product Designer (UX/UI) based in London, United Kingdom, with 8 years of experience in user interfaces, interaction design, and front-end development.

My natural curiosity drives me to continuously explore, investigate, and learn, which has led me on a fascinating journey from architecture to industrial design, culminating in my passion for digital product design. This non-linear career path has not only broadened my skill set but also experience design from various perspectives, advocating for the crucial role of the design process in product development.

Throughout my career, I've built a multidisciplinary skill set, actively participating in every stage of the product development lifecycle. From immersing myself in user research and collaborative workshops during the conceptualisation phase to delivering meticulously crafted interface designs, design systems, and prototypes.

Currently, I'm focused within the startup ecosystem, where I'm leading and delivering end-to-end digital products.
